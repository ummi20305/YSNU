Our Mission

Our goal from this website is to help reach closer to the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 2 of Zero Hunger, 
        this goal is to end hunger and ensure access of nutritious and sufficient food for all.  
        United Prosperity World is a website aiming to help educate, empower and support the public in helping to achieve Zero Hunger. 
        We believe that hunger is not just a challenge to overcome but an injustice that demands action. 
        Through the use of this website we will help get closer to our sustainable goal of ending world hunger.

United Nations Sustainable Developmeng Goals

The United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) are a set of 17 global goals adopted in 2015 to address:
        social, economic, and environmental challenges worldwide by 2030.
